Plant Watering & Care

General Instructions:

Click on a plant to see details. Use the colored border to track watering:

  • Green: Recently watered, likely okay.
  • Yellow: Getting close, check soil soon.
  • Red: Likely overdue for watering!

Symbols: 🩹 = Sick Plant | 🔔 = Beeps (Moisture Meter) | 🐌 = Poor Drainage (Water Sparingly) | ✨ = Other Special Care Needed

Bottom Watering (for 4" nursery pots): Water for 30 mins with tepid/warm water. Change water if it turns brown. (Cold water can shock plants).

IMPORTANT: Colors are estimates based on last watering. Always check the soil moisture before watering, especially for plants marked '🐌' (poor drainage) or plants marked as SICK '🩹'! Conditions change.
