Plant Watering & Care

General Instructions:

Click on a plant to see details. Use the colored border to track watering:

  • Green:

    Recently watered.

  • Yellow:

    Getting close, check soil soon.

  • Red:

    Needs Water!

Symbols:

🩹 = Sick Plant

🔔 = Beeps (Moisture Meter)

🐌 = Poor Drainage (Water Sparingly)

✨ = Other Special Care Needed

Bottom Watering (for 4" nursery pots): Place plant in cup of Water for 30 mins with tepid/warm water (Cold water can shock plants). Change water if it turns brown, Refill when empty.

IMPORTANT: Colors are estimates based on last watering, weather Conditions change.

Always check the soil moisture before watering, especially for plants marked '🐌' or '🩹'!
